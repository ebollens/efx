Demo: Tabs

The tabs library provides a target-trigger interaction whereby, when one of the triggering states is pressed, the target becomes visible and all others are hidden. With the tabs library, it is possible to achieve effects like an accordion.

<div data-target="ex1">
    <!-- Trigger 1 --></div>
<div data-target="ex2">
    <!-- Trigger 2 --></div>
<div data-effect="tabs">
    <div id="ex1" data-tabs="show">
        <!-- Target 1 --></div>
    <div id="ex2">
        <!-- Target 2 --></div>
</div>
