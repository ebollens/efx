Demo: Accordion

The accordion library provides a target-trigger interaction whereby, when one of the triggering states is pressed, all targets except for the trigger will be hidden.

<div data-effect="accordion">
    <div data-target="ex1">
        <!-- Trigger 1 --></div>
    <div id="ex1">
        <!-- Target 1 --></div>
    <div data-target=".ex2">
        <!-- Trigger 2 --></div>
    <div class="ex2">
        <!-- Target 2 --></div>
    <div class="ex2">
        <!-- Target 2 --></div>
    <div data-target="ex3">
        <!-- Trigger 3 --></div>
    <div id="ex3">
        <!-- Target 3 --></div>
</div>
